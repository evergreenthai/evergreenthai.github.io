Our Story

Every Fla vor Tells a Story

At Evergreen Thai Restaurant, every dish is crafted with love, tradition, and a passion for authentic Thai cuisine. Founded in 2007, Evergreen Thai Restaurant has been a beloved part of the Toronto community for over a decade. Known for our commitment to fresh ingredients, an MSG-free menu, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere, we take pride in delivering a dining experience that's both delicious and healthy.
