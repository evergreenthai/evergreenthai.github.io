Our Story

Every Flavor Tells a Story

At Evergreen Thai Kitchen & Bar, we bring Thai-inspired comfort food to downtown Toronto with a chill, local vibe. Founded in 2007 as Evergreen Thai Restaurant, we’ve been serving the community for years with fresh ingredients, an MSG-free menu, and great value — perfect for dine-in, takeout, and delivery.
