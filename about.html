Who we are

Evergreen Thai Restaurant offers delicious, healthy, home-style Thai dining and takeout to Toronto, ON.

Founded in 2007 by mother and son duo, Grace Guo and Peter Guo, Evergreen Thai Restaurant has been recognized for its fresh ingredients, entirely MSG-free menu, convenience, and an always-smiling staff.

Your time is valuable to us. Evergreen Thai Restaurant is known for its convenient "30 minutes to your car" takeout. Simply place your order online or via phone, arrive in 30 minutes, and our staff will bring your order to your car.
