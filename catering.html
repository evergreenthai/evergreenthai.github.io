Corporate & Office Thai Catering in Downtown Toronto

Located at 175 Dundas St W in downtown Toronto, Evergreen Thai Kitchen & Bar provides reliable Thai catering for government offices, corporate headquarters, downtown condo residences, University of Toronto events and Toronto Metropolitan University events.

We specialize in office lunches, business meetings, weekend overtime team meals, corporate gatherings, and large group orders. Free delivery is available for catering orders of 10+ people or a minimum of $200 CAD within a 5km radius. Advance notice of 24 hours is recommended (48+ hours for holidays and peak dates). For condo deliveries, orders are typically handed off at the lobby/front desk. If unit delivery is required, please inform us in advance. Vegetarian options are available. Our menu is MSG-free. Please notify us of any allergies when ordering.
