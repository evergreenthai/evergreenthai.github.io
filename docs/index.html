Restaurants

Make it easier for consumers to decide what to order at your restaurant! Restaurant users can edit their menu items, add new menu, and view their menu. Your menu will live in the JustMenu database, making it easy for consumers to find your most popular items!

Consumers

Not sure what to order? Sign up, and search through the JustMenu database for items based on keyword, view the menu of restaurants registered in the system, see reviews for specific menu items, and submit reviews for specific menu items.

JustMenu is a restaurant information web app for consumers that emphasizes well-indexed menu data and the ability to review individual menu items instead of just restaurants as a whole.
