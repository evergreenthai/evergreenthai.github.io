How you can work with us

Cook

Stir-fry / small pan cooking • Full-time • On-site (Downtown Toronto)

CA$17.20–CA$25.00 / hour Kitchen Team 175 Dundas St W

Evergreen Thai Kitchen & Bar is seeking a dedicated, detail-oriented cook to join our culinary team. Wok skills are welcome but not required.

Responsibilities

  • Prepare and cook dishes based on recipes and menu standards
  • Cook with a small pan; stir-fry/wok skills are an asset
  • Assist with prep (chopping, slicing, marinating)
  • Follow food handling and health & safety regulations
  • Maintain cleanliness and organization of the kitchen
  • Support timely service during peak hours
  • Follow proper food storage to maintain freshness and reduce waste

Requirements

  • Restaurant / food service experience preferred
  • Comfortable in a fast-paced environment
  • Good teamwork and communication
  • Mandarin communication is a strong asset
  • Valid Canadian work permit, PR, or citizenship

Benefits

  • Discounted or free food
